zev · the sounder · ♓ pisces

sound — charms into forms.

zev is the sounder: sound reads bytes (charms) into forms — a parser-combinator vocabulary of tiny functions that compose. a literal, a char, a run; choice, sequence, repetition — nothing alone, a grammar once you stack them. lifted from cook's Makefile importer so the whole crew can reuse it.

zev is the character-arm of ev = feel ∘ sound (= wev ∘ zev) — the two input lanes, characters and data, meet at one core via the copairing [sound, id]. Ze'ev the wolf hunts in a pack, the shape of the thing.
