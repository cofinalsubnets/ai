wev · the spinner · ♍ virgo

feel — spin prepared source.

wev is the spinner: feel is the source prepass — macroexpand, boxfix, fold pure global applications ((+ 1 2) → 3), mark apply strategy. it spins read source into prepared source, then grows the same machinery into a partial evaluator: specialize on what you know, leave the rest open.

wev is the data-arm of ev = feel ∘ sound (= wev ∘ zev) — the spinner after zev the sounder. both were lifted from the same place, the compiler's own gut. it reduces what it can see and stops honestly at what it cannot; a fold that changes a meaning is a bug, not a saving.
