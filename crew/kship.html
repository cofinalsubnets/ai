kship · the ship · ♒ aquarius (first guess)

the language, perceiving, deciding, acting on bare metal.

kship is the ship the whole crew rides — the freestanding ai kernel grown a network stack and a persistent agent loop: it perceives (network and clock), decides (a policy — that is tele at the helm), and acts (the wire and spawned tasks), unattended, on real hardware. no Linux, no libc.

not a greenfield OS: the substrate was mostly here — the egg, the scheduler, the condition system. kship is the kernel grown a NIC and an agent loop, and the perceive → decide → act fold never returns. the others are its systems — aineko the net, bao the shell, gwen the cartographer of names — and kship is the hull. a watchdog with a face keeps it on course: a fault welps and the loop carries the last good state forward.
